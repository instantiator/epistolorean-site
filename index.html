The Epistolorean

Epistolary books are written as letters or journals. They tell a story through the eyes the characters, capturing their thoughts, feelings, and events as and when they are recorded.

The Epistolorean brings your favourite epistolary books to life. You'll receive emails from the characters in the story, as they write their journals and letters.
